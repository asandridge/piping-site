The best known of all bagpipes, the Great Highland Bagpipes of Scotland are what most people think of when they hear the word "bagpipes". They are played standing up with the pipes resting on the shoulder, and are most often seen in weddings and funerals, as well as many a Saint Patrick's Day parade.

Most bagpipe performances are done in standard highland wear, including kilt, vest, socks, and shoes. They can, however, be performed in whichever dress the client desires.

Favorites on the pipes are Amazing Grace and Scotland the Brave.
