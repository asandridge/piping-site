Uilleann (il-an) Pipes are Ireland's national bagpipes, as opposed to the far better known Scottish Highland Bagpipes. Uilleann pipes are much quieter, and are more suitable for the indoors. They also have a larger scale, and play well with other instruments like guitar, accordion, or flute. Instead of standing, an uilleann piper plays sitting down, the pipes resting on the lap. They will sound familiar to those who have seen movies like Braveheart or Titanic. They are great for any kind of event, including weddings, funerals, teas, clan gatherings, anniversaries, and parties.
